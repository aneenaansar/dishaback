Counselling Psychologist

Hello, and welcome to my counselling centre. My name is Rema Devi, and I am a counselling psychologist with a Ph.D. degree in Counselling Psychology. I have over 30 years of experience in teaching and counselling, and I am passionate about helping people achieve their personal and professional goals.

I started my counselling centre at home in 2014, and since then I have been providing professional and compassionate counselling services for individuals, couples, families, and groups. I offer a range of counselling services, including pre-marital counselling, family counselling/therapy, child & adolescent counselling, school counselling, group counselling, awareness programs, motivational sessions, career guidance, teacher development programmes, and grief therapy.
